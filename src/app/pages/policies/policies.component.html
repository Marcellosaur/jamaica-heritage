Privacy Policy and Accessibility Statement

Effective date: February 24, 2026

Privacy Policy

Swamp Safari is committed to protecting your personal information and handling it responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information such as your email address and encrypted password hash.
  • Order-related information such as selected products, quantities, and order totals.
  • Basic technical data required for website operation and security.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Process cart and checkout actions.
  • Maintain platform security and prevent misuse.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.

3. Payment Information

When you complete a purchase, we collect payment card information required to authorize and process your transaction, including cardholder name, card number, expiration date, and security code.

Payment information is processed using secure transport encryption and is used solely for payment authorization, fraud prevention, transaction settlement, and related customer support.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is disclosed only when required by law, or when necessary to protect the security and integrity of the website and its users.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to protect stored information, including secure password hashing and authenticated access controls.

6. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Requests can be submitted through the website Contact page.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Accessibility Statement

Swamp Safari is committed to providing a website experience that is accessible to all users, including people with disabilities.

We continuously work to support accessibility by:

  • Using clear page structure, headings, and labels.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation for core interactions.
  • Maintaining legible text and usable color contrast.
  • Providing meaningful alternative text for key imagery.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please contact us through the Contact page with details of the issue and affected page.
