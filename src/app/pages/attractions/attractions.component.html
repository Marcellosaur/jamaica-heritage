Planning Your Visit

Opening Hours

  • Monday – Saturday: 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.
  • Sunday & Public Holidays: 10:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.
  • Last tour usually begins 1 hour before closing.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes or sandals
  • Insect repellent and sunscreen
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Camera or smartphone for photos

Safety Tips

  • Stay behind safety rails at all times.
  • Follow instructions from guides and handlers.
  • Do not attempt to feed or touch animals.
  • Children should remain with an adult.
